edindies - gamedev co-working

We're an open group of game developers in Edinburgh, meeting up for co-working, playtesting and chat.

We meet every Saturday from 2pm in the Summerhall Cafe, everyone welcome, just bring yourself and something to work on, experience optional.

You can get in touch on twitter or email.
